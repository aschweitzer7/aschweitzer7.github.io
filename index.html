Mobile App for Mental Health

    • Virtually no research exists examining the daily influence of environmental factors on the development of mental health disorders such as schizophrenia. Our team at Georgia Tech is working with Dr. Benson Ku, MD, a psychiatrist at Emory University, to do just this. Our solution is a mobile application that collects real-time data on the status of the users' mental health such as their feelings and emotions in relation to their location during the day. We can compile and organize this data for our client and other clinicians to analyze and have the ability to better treat their own patients and gain knowledge of the development of mental health disorders.
